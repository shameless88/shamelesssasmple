Perfect for small businesses and startups needing a professional online presence.

Includes:

  • Custom-designed 5-page website (Home, About, Services, Blog, Contact)
  • Responsive design for all devices
  • Basic SEO setup (meta tags, sitemap submission)
  • Contact form integration
  • 1-month post-launch support

"Get online quickly with a thoughtfully crafted website designed to establish your brand and engage your visitors."

2. Growth Package

Price: $4,000 – $7,000 (fixed or hourly)

Ideal for businesses looking to scale, enhance user experience, and include more features.

"Expand your online potential with a dynamic website designed to attract, inform, and convert your target audience."

  • Training and documentation

"Empower your business with a robust, scalable web solution designed to deliver exceptional user experiences and drive growth."
